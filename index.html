About Me

Technology has always fascinated me and despite a thriving career in healthcare administration, I've spent the last 8 years studying a wide assortment of technologies that include Cloud Computing, Digital Media, Enterprise Architecture, Data Analytics, IT Project Management, Interactive Design, and Software and Web Development; earning an A.S. in Information Technology, a B.S. in Information Systems Management, and most recently a certification in UX/UI Development.

My desire has always been to help people. Assisting patients in navigating a complex health system has given me great joy and job satisfaction over the years.

With the onset of the pandemic, a wave of new technologies in healthcare needed to see patients remotely and monitor those with the most critical diagnosis utilizing remote monitoring systems have been made mainstream. However, the patients needing the assistance of the new technologies the most which include the elderly and those with mental and physical impairments as well as those affected by social factors and educational inequalities are struggling with these new means of obtaining healthcare.

I am passionate about technology and services designed with a people-centered approach. I specialize in creating usable digital products that help others feel included and valued. UX Design is a human-centered approach that relies heavily on empathy and psychology; has the potential to save lives and deliver a positive social impact.

My greatest aspiration is to help make digital products and technology usable, enjoyable, and accessible to everyone.
